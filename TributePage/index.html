Nikola Tesla

The man who electrified the world

Timeline of Tesla's Life

  • 1856 - Born in Smiljan, Austrian Empire (modern-day Croatia)
  • 1875 - Enrolled at Austrian Polytechnic in Graz
  • 1882 - Conceptualized the induction motor
  • 1884 - Immigrated to the United States
  • 1888 - Patented the AC induction motor and transformer
  • 1891 - Invented the Tesla coil
  • 1893 - Demonstrated wireless power transmission
  • 1895 - Designed one of the first AC hydroelectric power plants at Niagara Falls
  • 1943 - Died in New York City at age 86
"The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked, is mine."
