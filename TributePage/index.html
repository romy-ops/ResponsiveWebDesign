Nikola Tesla

⚡ The man who electrified the world ⚡

🚀 Timeline of Tesla's Life

  • 1856 – Born in Smiljan, Austrian Empire
  • 1875 – Enrolled at Austrian Polytechnic in Graz
  • 1882 – Conceptualized the induction motor
  • 1884 – Immigrated to the United States
  • 1888 – Patented the AC induction motor
  • 1891 – Invented the Tesla coil
  • 1893 – Demonstrated wireless power transmission
  • 1895 – Built AC hydroelectric plant at Niagara Falls
  • 1943 – Passed away in New York City
